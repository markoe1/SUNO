# YouTube Batch Upload Strategy

## 🎯 Core Principle

```
Title Hook → Description CTA → Tags → Privacy → UPLOAD
```

One JSON file. Infinite scale.

---

## 📝 Viral Title Formulas (Pick One Per Video)

### Formula 1: Curiosity + Payoff
```
"This AI song is [adjective] — wait for the drop"
```
Examples:
- "This AI song is insane — wait for the drop"
- "This AI beat shouldn't exist — wait for it"
- "This AI music goes harder than real artists — wait"

**Why it works:** Curiosity + promise of payoff

---

### Formula 2: Comparison/Shock
```
"I made this in [timeframe] with AI (it's [result])"
```
Examples:
- "I made this in 10 seconds with AI (it slaps)"
- "I made this in 1 minute with AI (better than Spotify)"
- "I made this with AI (no human touch)"

**Why it works:** Disbelief + accessibility

---

### Formula 3: Challenge to Human Artists
```
"[Human artist comparison] but it's AI-generated"
```
Examples:
- "This sounds like a Weeknd track but it's AI"
- "This is Daft Punk level but made by AI"
- "This could fool a music producer — it's AI"

**Why it works:** Competitive tension + skill validation

---

### Formula 4: Speed/Efficiency
```
"AI just [achievement] in [timeframe]"
```
Examples:
- "AI just created a billboard-ready track in 30 seconds"
- "AI just made a complete song in 1 minute"
- "AI just beat a music producer in a speed challenge"

**Why it works:** Impressive vs traditional process

## 🔐 Privacy Strategy

| Status | Use Case | Best For |
|--------|----------|----------|
| `unlisted` | Testing hooks | First 3-5 videos |
| `private` | Internal review | Quality checks |
| `public` | Scale winners | Videos with proven engagement |

**Recommendation:**
- Start with `unlisted` (visible by link)
- Monitor watch time
- Go `public` once you see engagement

## 📊 Tracking Metadata (For Analysis)

Add to each video in JSON:
```json
{
  "file_path": "clips/song1.mp4",
  "title": "...",
  "test_cohort": "Curiosity Hook v1",
  "expected_audience": "Electronic music fans",
  "success_metric": "Watch time > 30s"
}
```

This helps you measure:
- Which title formulas work
- Which genres get traction
- Which hooks convert to subscribers

## 🚀 Launch Sequence

### Phase 1: Test (5 videos, unlisted)
- Mix of title formulas
- Real mood/genre variation
- Monitor watch time daily
- Track which descriptions get comments

### Phase 2: Scale Winners (10-15 videos, public)
- Double down on winning hook
- Similar genre/vibe
- Push to subscribers
- Drive traffic to best performers

### Phase 3: Algorithmic (Full catalog, optimized)
- All 18+ videos live
- Pinned highest-performing
- Playlists by mood
- Cross-promotion
