How SUNO Works

1. Discover Campaigns

SUNO scans Whop for active campaigns. Pick which ones you want to clip from — set your preferences and let the daemon run.

2. Auto-Clip Videos

Automatically downloads videos from campaign sources (Google Drive, YouTube) and creates short clips optimized for each platform.

3. Post Everywhere

Posts clips to TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts 24/7. Manages account warmup and posting limits to avoid bans.

4. Track Earnings

Monitors view counts and calculates earnings. Dashboard shows real-time stats: clips posted, views, earnings, account health.

5. Submit for Payment

Submits posted clip URLs to Whop. Whop pays out based on views. You earn $3–$5 per 1,000 views depending on campaign.

6. Reinvest or Withdraw

Earnings go to your Whop account. Reinvest in more content, or withdraw to your bank. Fully automated pipeline.

Key Questions

Is this legal?

Posting to platforms using automation violates their Terms of Service. Your accounts may be suspended or banned. SUNO is a tool — how you use it is your responsibility. We recommend starting with test accounts and understanding the risks.

How much can I earn?

Earnings depend on video views and campaign CPM. Expect $3–$5 per 1,000 views. If you post 100 clips/month averaging 10K views each, that's roughly $3,000–$5,000/month. Your results will vary based on content quality and audience.

Do I need a Whop account?

Yes. SUNO automates posting for Whop campaigns you've joined. You need an existing Whop account with campaign access to get started.

Will my accounts get banned?

Possibly. Platform automation is risky. SUNO includes account warmup features and rate limiting to reduce bans, but there's no guarantee. Start with test accounts and scale gradually.
